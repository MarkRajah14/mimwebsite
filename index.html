Welcome To Minorities In Medicine

Mission:

The mission of this organization is to advance education and science by educating Jersey City youth about COVID-19, Health Disparities, Health Literacy, Public Health Policy, Anatomy, Medical Procedures, the path to becoming a professional in medicine, and Teen Mental Health.
